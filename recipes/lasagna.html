Lasagna

Lasagna just like Nona used to make

Lasagna is a wonderfully Italian dish which is prepared by laying delicious ingredients to equal more than the sum of their parts

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of Sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 12oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Method

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch ovenover med heat until browned
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Boli a large pot of water. Cook lasagna in boiling water for 8-10 minutes, drain and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a bowl, combine the ricotta with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  6. Preheat oven to 190C
  7. Assemble in layers
  8. Top with parmesan and foil to stop it burning
  9. Bake for 25 minutes covered, then 25 minutes uncovered
  10. Rest Lasagna for 15 minutes before serving
