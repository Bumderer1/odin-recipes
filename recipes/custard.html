Custard

Who doesn't like a good homemade custard?

Follow along below and you too could have a warm bowl of sweet hugs. Just add banana, or a slice of ginger cake for a great winter pudding.

Ingredients

  • 200ml Double Cream
  • 700ml Whole Milk
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 3 tbsp Cornflour
  • 100g Caster Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Method

  1. Put cream and milk in a pan and gently bring to the boil
  2. Whisk the egg yolks, cornflour, sugar, and vanilla
  3. Gradually pour the hot milk mixture onto the sugar mixture, whisking constantly
  4. Wipe out the pan and pour the mixture back into it
  5. Heat gently, stirring with a wooden spoon until custard is thickened, but before any lumps form
  6. Serve hot or cold
