Roasted Cheese

Cheese on Toast or Roasted Cheese?

whatever you call it, you are only ever a few grinds of balck pepper away from cheesy heaven.

Ingredients

  • A slice of bread
  • A slice of cheese
  • A few turns of a black pepper grinder

Method

  1. Place the bread under the grill until it is toasted on one side
  2. Flip the bread over and cover most of the surface of the slice with cheese
  3. Place back under the grill until the cheese is bubbling and the crust has started to burn
